Best Burger Sauce

Description

This burger sauce has a zippy flavor that goes well in sandwiches or as a fry dip. It's a tangy sauce that can be made spicy or milder for kids.

Ingredients

These are the simple ingredients you’ll need to make this restaurant-worthy burger sauce:

  • Condiments: A flavorful mixture of ketchup, mayonnaise, and mustard is the base.
  • Seasonings: Dried minced onion, dried minced garlic, and seasoned pepper.
  • Hot sauce: To taste, for spiciness.
  • Vinegar: A dash of vinegar adds zing and balances flavors.

Exact Measurements

For 1x serving:


  • 1 cup mayonnaise
  • ½ cup ketchup
  • ¼ cup yellow mustard
  • 2 teaspoons dried minced onion
  • ¼ teaspoon dried minced garlic
  • ⅛ teaspoon white vinegar
  • Hot sauce (to taste)
  • Seasoned pepper (to taste)

Directions

Making burger sauce is quick and easy. Here’s what to do:

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Whisk together mayonnaise, ketchup, mustard, onion, garlic, and vinegar in a bowl.
  3. Season with hot sauce and seasoned pepper.
  4. Cover and chill for at least 1 hour before serving.
  5. Serve and enjoy!
