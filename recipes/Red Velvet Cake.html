Red Velvet Cake

Description

This red velvet cake recipe is a family favorite. A classic cake for the Christmas holiday!

Red Velvet Cake Ingredients

These are the ingredients you'll need to make this top-rated Red Velvet Cake recipe:

Ingredients

For 1x serving

  • 1 ½ cups white sugar
  • ½ cup shortening
  • 2 eggs
  • 4 tablespoons red food coloring
  • 2 tablespoons cocoa
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 ½ cups sifted all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon distilled white vinegar
  • 1 ½ teaspoons baking soda

Icing:

  • 1 cup milk
  • 5 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup butter, room temperature
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions

How to Make Red Velvet Cake

Making Red Velvet Cake much easier than it looks. You'll find the full, step-by-step recipe below — but here's a brief overview of what you can expect:

  1. Step 1: Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease two 9-inch round pans.
  2. Step 2: Make the cake: Beat 1 1/2 cups sugar and shortening together in a large bowl with an electric mixer until light and fluffy. Add eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition. Combine red food coloring and cocoa to make a paste; add to creamed mixture.
  3. Step 3: Mix buttermilk, salt, and 1 teaspoon vanilla together in a small bowl. Add flour, alternating with buttermilk mixture, mixing just until incorporated.
  4. Step 4: Mix vinegar and baking soda together; gently fold into cake batter and pour into prepared pans.
  5. Step 5: Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean, about 30 minutes. Cool on a wire rack for 5 minutes. Run a table knife around the edges to loosen. Invert carefully onto a serving plate or cooling rack. Let cool, about 30 minutes.
  6. Step 6: Make the icing: Heat milk and flour in a saucepan over low heat, stirring constantly, until thick. Set aside to cool completely.
  7. Step 7: Meanwhile, beat sugar, butter, and vanilla together in a large bowl with an electric mixer until light and fluffy. Add cooled flour mixture and beat until frosting is a good spreading consistency. Frost cake layers when completely cool.
