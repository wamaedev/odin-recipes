Lemon Blueberry Pound Cake

Description

This lemon blueberry pound cake is a tangy variation of an old blueberry pound cake recipe with the bright flavor of lemon. I love it and so do my kids!

Lemon Blueberry Pound Cake Ingredients

These are the ingredients you'll need to make this Lemon Blueberry Pound Cake:

Ingredients

For 1x serving

  • 3 cups white sugar
  • 2 cups butter, softened
  • 6 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons lemon extract
  • 4 cups unbleached all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon grated lemon zest
  • 1 cup milk, room temperature
  • 2 cups fresh blueberries

Directions

How to Make Lemon Blueberry Pound Cake

Making Lemon Blueberry Pound Cake much easier than it looks. You'll find the full, step-by-step recipe below â€” but here's a brief overview of what you can expect:

  1. Step 1:Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease and flour a 10-inch Bundt pan.
  2. Step 2:Beat sugar and butter together in a large bowl with an electric mixer until light in color and fluffy, about 5 to 7 minutes. Beat in eggs, one at a time, then stir in lemon extract.
  3. Step 3:Combine flour, baking powder, and lemon zest; gradually stir into cake batter alternating with milk. I like to use a spatula and stir by hand, mixing just until blended so the batter is not over mixed, scraping the bottom and sides of the bowl often. Gently fold in blueberries; spoon batter into the prepared pan.
  4. Step 4:Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean, about 1 hour to 1 hour 20 minutes. Let cool in the pan for at least 10 minutes, then invert onto a wire rack to cool completely.
